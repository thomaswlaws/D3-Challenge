Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

Exploration for the association between different health factors like (lack of healthcare, obesity and smoking) vs different factors like poverty, age, income. Analysis has been done to see the correlation between the different variables. Data has been collected from the American Community Survey (ACS) and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) during the year of 2014. Each circle represents US state.

Interactive plot shows different data views. To see the relationship between different factors chose the particular axis.
For example, Considering the relationship between Poverty and Lack of healthcare, it has been seen that TX has maximum % without healthcare whereas Massachusetts has lowest % without healthcare.

To see the details of the chosen factors for particular state, hover over the state circle.
